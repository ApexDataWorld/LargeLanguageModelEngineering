# Faculty Profile: Dr. Wanda Maximoff

## Summary
- **Date of Birth:** March 12, 1975
- **Department:** Computer Science
- **Current Position:** Associate Professor
- **Location:** Stanford, California

## Academic & Professional Progression
- **1997:** Graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science from UCLA.
- **2001:** Completed M.S. in Computer Science at MIT.
- **2006:** Earned Ph.D. in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University.
- **2007:** Joined Stanford University as an Assistant Professor.
- **2013:** Promoted to Associate Professor after significant contributions in AI research.

## Teaching & Research
- **Courses Taught:** CS101 - Introduction to Computer Science, CS205 - Data Structures and Algorithms.
- **Research Interests:** Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computational Neuroscience.
- **Publications:** Authored over 50 peer-reviewed papers in leading journals.
- **Awards:** Recipient of the NSF Early Career Award.

## Other Notes
- Actively mentors graduate students.
- Leads several interdisciplinary research projects in collaboration with industry partners.
- Passionate about integrating research into undergraduate education.
